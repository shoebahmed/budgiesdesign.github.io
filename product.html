Ben Bridge Jeweler

Ben Bridge needed a digital partner to create an eCommerce presence that would have a more serious impact on their overall business.

Design & Development

Fresh Consulting created a mobile version of the Ben Bridge website, cleaned up the layout, optimized navigation, simplified the checkout path, and incorporated many new features such as PayPal and Amazon Checkout integration to support online sales. Fresh also supported multiple seasonal campaigns with unique landing pages, banners, and ads to deliver their message.
